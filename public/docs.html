Introduction

Welcome to the Resume Builder documentation. This guide will help you navigate through the process of creating a professional resume using our website

Getting Started

To begin building your resume, follow these steps:

  1. Register for an account or log in if you already have one.
  2. Click on the 'Create New Resume' button to start a new project.
  3. Choose a template that suits your industry and style preferences.

Editing Your Resume

Our editor is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly. Here's how you can edit your resume:

  • To add text, click on any text area and start typing.
  • Use the toolbar to format text, add bullet points, and more.
  • Drag and drop sections to rearrange the layout of your resume.

Adding Sections

You can add custom sections to your resume by following these steps:

Click the 'Add Section' button and select the type of section you want to include, such as Education, Experience, or Skills.

Customizing the Design

Customize the look and feel of your resume with these options:

  • Change the color scheme by selecting from our color palette.
  • Choose different fonts for headings and body text.
  • Adjust the layout by selecting a one-column or two-column design.

Saving and Exporting

Once you're satisfied with your resume, you can save or export it:

  • Click the 'Save' button to save your progress.
  • Use the 'Export' button to download your resume as a PDF or Word document.
